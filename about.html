What we do

We are fully aware of the diverse environmental challenges in a vast territory like the Nigerian environment, so we operate in total compliance with local, International, statutory and regulatory/legislative standard to bring positive impact to the environment. As a promoter of local content, we strongly uphold the practice of transfer of skills/knowledge and capacity with certifications to indigenous labor force as we deliver a world class quality and timely result in all our projects. To achieve all these, we deploy state-of-the-art expertise and equipment on all projects, partnering with over 18 internationally viable and competent companies with global record in all their operations. In all our sensitive and major projects we align with our international partners and collaborate with them to execute such project with their full technical support and expertise.

GIOLEE VISION:To be the biggest environmental company in Nigeria/Africa and one of the best in the world.

MISSION: Our mission is to play globally and act locally to create a world class environmental solution by offering excellent values for money in order to achieve and maintain a reputation in supplying of environmental and allied services to our clients.

COMPANY PHILOSOPHY: To be the leading environmental service company of excellence by providing professional services and promoting second to none environmental and allied services in Nigeria/Africa and by extension a key player on the global stage.
